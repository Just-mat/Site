Presentation of the EOS project

Project selection

We have chosen to create an application that can recognize facial expressions of people. It would thus be a question of detecting the faces as well as determining if the people are sad, happy, etc...

The decomposition of our project

We will break down our project into 4 main areas: Image processing, Detection of Facial Features, Emotion Detection, Interface.

Image processing: image processing will allow us to optimize the image as best as possible provided, for example calibrate the face in the center of the image

Feature detection: this part consists in detecting the features of the faces to then create a black and white image (black background and facial features are white) used for emotion inference. emotion inference.

Emotion inference: we will use a neural network to infer the emotion from the face in the photo.

Interface: this part is for now the most unclear, indeed we do not know yet exactly what display we will use to return the results of our program.

Interest of the project

In today's world, where social interactions are increasingly taking place online, it is important to understand how others feel and to effectively communicate our own emotions. That's why our application aims to help users better understand and manage their emotions and improve their social interactions.
